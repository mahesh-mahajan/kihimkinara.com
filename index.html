Best Quality Stay and Food at Kihim Beach

"Kinara" is a beautiful cottage at Kihim beach is a place for relaxation with your loved ones, family and friends. The property is situated right on the beach of Kihim which is a coastal village near Alibaug, Maharashtra. This is one of the ideal place to plan an overnight weekend or a one day picnic from Mumbai and Pune. We have well appointed two private rooms and two spacious dormitory rooms where we can offer best options to stay for our guest.
We cater our guests with home cooked, authentic Konkani and Maharashtrian styled vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals prepared with attention and love, though our speciality is in sea food. Our preparation of prawn, fish, crab and lobster will ignite your taste buds.

Near places of interest.

There are several things you can do in and around Kihim and Alibaug, very few are listed below. Ask us to get insider news and latest updates

Kolaba Fort

Sea Fort built by Shivaji Maharaj situated right in front of Alibaug beach around 1 kilometer deep in sea.
14 to 15 KM from Kinara.

Janjira Fort

This unconquered sea fort situated in Murud. Don't forget to checkout 3rd largest canon in India named Kalal Bangdi.
60 to 65 KM from Kinara.

Korlai Fort and Light House

This fort is built by the Portugese, Korlai fort and lighthouse offer panoramic views of the sea.
30 KM from Kinara en-route to Murud.

Birla Temple

Vikram Ispat company, a part of Birla Udyog Group has built this temple. On entering the temple there is a square shaped nave.
35 KM from Kinara.

Nagaon Beach

Some of you might still remember Dimple in swim suit in "Saagar" movie, that famous song ;) that was shot here.
18 to 20 KM from Kinara.

Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary

This is a wildlife refuge in the Murud. Home of several species of birds and animals such as kite, hornbill, wild boars, etc.
40 KM from Kinara.
