    Best food and stay to make your beach weekend memorable!

    There are numerous bed and breakfasts in Alibag, and then there's Kinara Homestay. At Kinara, you don't just get a bed and food, but you are welcomed into a beautiful Konkani family where you can stay at the recently renovated beachfront cottage and get an unforgettable gastronomic experience.

    The property is situated right on the beach of Kihim is a small coastal village located north of Alibag, well connected by roads and waterways. Maharashtra’s first Ro-Pax ferry service M2M Ferries, between Bhaucha Dhakka and Mandwa Jetty, makes it possible to reach Kihim from South Mumbai within 90 minutes with your vehicle. This makes it one of the ideal destinations for overnight or one-day excursions.

    Unique Selling Points of our offerings are; The homestay location, where you are always just 10 steps away from the beach and the home-cooked authentic Maharashtrian and Konkani-styled food prepared with love and attention. Our preparation of prawns, fish, and crabs will ignite your taste buds. If you are coming for the first time to "Kinara", here is a pro tip for you: "Don't forget to ask for Sol Kadhi for lunch." :)

    We have following arrangements to cater our guests.

    • 2 double bedded air-conditioned private rooms with sea view on the upper deck to cater to families.
    • 2 dormitory rooms on the lower deck with access to the patio can accommodate a group of 8 to 12 pax.
    • Authentic & fresh home-cooked Konkani and Maharashtrian-styled non-vegetarian and vegetarian meals.

    Ruins of forts tales epics of a fight for freedom in the past

    Khanderi & Underi 10 minutes Boat ride to twin sea forts. 5 KM from Kinara.

    Kolaba Fort Visiting hours may vary depending tide. 15 KM from Kinara.

    Revdanda Fort Located at the mouth of the Kundalika River. 30 KM from Kinara.

    Korlai Fort Amazing seascape from the top of the fort. 35 KM from Kinara.

    Janjira Fort Fisherman built this fort in 12th century. 65 KM from Kinara.

    Temple going is for the purification of the soul

    Datta Mandir Ancient and prominent hill top temple. 15 KM from Kinara.

    Birla Temple Birla Udyog Group has built this temple. 35 KM from Kinara.

    Kankeshwar Temple Must visit temple of lord Shiva. 8 KM from Kinara.

    Portuguese Church Remains of an old church in Revdanda. 30 KM from Kinara.

    Magen Aboth Synagogue Prior arrangements required. 12 KM from Kinara.

    Look deep into nature, and you will understand everything better

    Phansad Sanctuary Coastal Woodland Ecosystem of Western Ghats. 55 KM from Kinara.

    Native Biodiversity Garden A must visit treasure well worth spending an afternoon. 15 KM from Kinara.

    Akshi Beach & Forest Great place for Birdwatching. 18 KM from Kinara.

    The best introduction to Art & History is to stroll through a museum

    Karmarkar Museum of Sculpture A small but amazing sculpture meusium created to experience beautiful artwork of Padmashree VP Karmarkar. 11 KM from Kinara.

    Dashrath Patel Museum the Museum showcases the works of Padmashri Dashrath Patel. He was the first director of education of The National Institute of Design. 3 KM from Kinara.

    Ashirwad Kala Dalan Great pieces of art built entirely from coconut shells. 12 KM from Kinara.

    If you want to learn about our World then learn Science

    Alibag Magnetic Observatory One of the oldest magnetic observatory of its type, still predicting accurate. 12 KM from Kinara.

    Korlai Lighthouse Old lighthous, one can get panoramic view of Arabian sea. 35 KM from Kinara.

    Kolgaon Lighthouse Old lighthous, difficult to climb need technical expertise. 14 KM from Kinara.
